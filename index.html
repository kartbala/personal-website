About

I'm an Assistant Professor of Information Systems and Supply Chain Management at Howard University School of Business, focused on real-world optimization.

My research explores base-of-the-pyramid business strategy in Sub-Saharan Africa and voter turnout among traditionally marginalized urban communities in the United States.

DBA from Harvard Business School (2018)
BS in Electrical Engineering and Economics from Duke University (2007)
