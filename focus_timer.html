About the Focus Timer

This timer uses the Pomodoro Technique - a productivity method that breaks work into focused intervals (traditionally 25 minutes) separated by short breaks.

How to use:

  • Choose your focus duration (25 minutes is the classic Pomodoro)
  • Click "Start" and work with full concentration
  • When the timer rings, take a 5-minute break
  • After 4 focus sessions, take a longer 15-30 minute break
  • Repeat to maintain sustained productivity

Benefits: Improved focus, reduced mental fatigue, better time estimation, and increased productivity through time-boxing.
